Faire

An apt-get frontend for the lazy.

Overview

Welcome to Faire. Faire is an apt-get frontend for the lazy. all you have to do is type in chmod +x install.sh && ./install.sh and the work is done. Now all you have to type is faire -i <package> and wait. Get a snack or go to the bathroom if you need. The work is being done for you. Look at this site. Look at how lazily this is made. This is the whole concept of Faire. To make everything as easy as possible.
